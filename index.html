Welcome to Paranaque City. This is the Web-Based System of Transparency Budget of Paranaque City.

Education

The education section provides information about the city's educational initiatives and programs. There were such initiatives to improve the quality of education in the city including school building projects, other structures and training & scholarship expenses. The education budget for year 2026 is Php. 600,000,000 Million.

Health

The health section provides information about the city's healthcare services and programs. In recent years, the city has implemented various health initiatives to improve the well-being of its residents, including dissemination of health information and the provision of medical supplies and equipment. The budget allocation for the health sector in 2026 is Php. 350,000,000 Million.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure section provides information about the city's development projects and improvements. The city has been investing in upgrading its roads, bridges, and public facilities to enhance the quality of life for its residents. The budget allocation for the infrastructure sector in 2026 is Php. 4,500,000,000 Billion.

Social Services

The social services section provides information about the city's programs and initiatives aimed at supporting its residents. These include assistance for vulnerable populations, community development projects, and social welfare programs. The budget allocation for social services in 2026 is Php. 4,500,000,000 Billion.

Economy

The economy section provides information about the city's economic initiatives and programs. The city has been focusing on promoting economic growth and development through various projects and investments. The budget allocation for the economy sector in 2026 is Php. 200,000,000 Million.
